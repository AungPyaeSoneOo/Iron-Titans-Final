Gym Diet Plan

Fuel Your Gains with the Right Nutrition

Macronutrient Goals

Hitting your macros is essential for muscle growth and recovery. Here's a balanced breakdown for active lifters:

Protein

1.6-2.2g

per kg body weight

Carbs

4-6g

per kg body weight

Fats

0.8-1g

per kg body weight

Top Protein Sources

  • Chicken Breast
  • Lean Beef
  • Salmon
  • Eggs
  • Greek Yogurt
  • Whey Protein
  • Cottage Cheese
  • Turkey

Carb Sources for Energy

  • Oatmeal
  • Brown Rice
  • Sweet Potatoes
  • Quinoa
  • Bananas
  • Whole Grain Bread

Meal Timing

Pre-Workout Carbs + moderate protein 1-2 hours before training
Post-Workout Fast protein + simple carbs within 30-60 minutes
Before Bed Slow-digesting protein like casein or cottage cheese

Hydration

Water is crucial for performance and recovery. Aim for at least 3-4 liters daily, more on training days.

Pro Tip Add electrolytes during intense workouts lasting over 60 minutes to maintain performance and prevent cramping.
